Station 1: Long Ball

Goal: Asymmetric dodgeball—sprint the gauntlet and survive.

Mechanics & Rules

  • Setup: 1 Offense vs. 1 Defense (8 players). Safe zones at both ends.
  • Offense throws the 1st ball into the field and sprints to the opposite safe zone.
  • The Twist: A 2nd ball in the center activates once the 1st ball hits the ground. Defense uses both!
  • Defense: Cannot move while holding a ball (1 pivot step max).

Swipe/Press DOWN for Scoring

Long Ball: Scoring Details

  • Scoring: 1 point every time a runner successfully returns to the STARTING safe zone without being hit.
  • Multiple Points: A single player can keep running back and forth to rack up points until they are tagged.
  • Elimination: You only sub out and go to the back of your team's line if you are hit by a ball.

Station 2: Chock Ball

Goal: Fast-paced, non-contact rebounding game.

Mechanics & Rules

  • No Blocking: Defense cannot block or intercept passes/shots. They stop points ONLY by catching rebounds.
  • Rule of 3s: Max 3 passes before shooting. Max 3 seconds holding the ball. Max 3 steps with the ball.
  • Frames: Teams can attack and score on BOTH frames. Max 3 consecutive shots per frame.

Swipe/Press DOWN for Scoring

Tchoukball: Scoring Details

  • How to Score: A player throws the ball at the rebound frame.
  • If the ball bounces off the frame and lands on the floor outside the forbidden zone before the defense catches it, it's 1 point!
  • If the defending team catches the rebound before it hits the floor, no points are scored and play continues.

Station 3: Chair Ball

Goal: Strategic passing and court awareness to reach the target.

Mechanics & Rules

  • Setup: Teams are scattered. One "Catcher" per team stands inside a hula hoop at opposite ends.
  • Objective: Score by successfully delivering the ball to your Catcher.
  • Blocking Rule: If a player tries to block a pass and knocks the ball to the ground, they must take possession of it.

Swipe/Press DOWN for the 3x3 Rule

Chair Ball: The 3x3 Rule

  • 3 Passes: Your team must complete at least 3 consecutive passes among yourselves before passing to the Catcher in the hula hoop.
  • 3 Steps: You cannot take more than 3 steps while holding the ball.
  • 3 Seconds: You cannot hold the ball in your hands for more than 3 seconds. Pass or lose possession!

Station 4: Dodge Medic

Goal: Tactical dodgeball—eliminate the enemy Medic to stop the reboots.

Mechanics & Rules

  • Setup: 5 balls in the center (2 on far right, 2 on far left, 1 in middle). Teams race to grab them.
  • The Twist: 1 Medic per team (secret identity). The Medic can heal any eliminated player.
  • Target the Medic! No one can revive the Medic once they are out.
  • Game continues until one team is completely eliminated.

Swipe/Press DOWN for Details

Dodge Medic: Details

  • Hits: Hit below the shoulders = out. If a player catches a thrown ball, the thrower is out, and a teammate comes back in.
  • Blocking: You can block with a ball, but if it knocks the ball out of your hands, you are out.
  • Boundaries: You cannot cross the center line. If you do, you are out.

Station 5: Golden Ball

Goal: Move the mass without entering the grid using coordinated force.

Mechanics & Rules

  • Setup: Teams stand outside on the four sides of a square perimeter.
  • A large Gym Ball (Golden Ball) is placed in the center.
  • Teams use smaller balls to hit and move the Golden Ball simultaneously.
  • Forbidden: No player can enter the square. All teammates must stand on the line.

Swipe/Press DOWN for Details

Golden Ball: Details

  • Scoring: When the Gym Ball goes out from one of the four sides, the team on that side loses a point.
  • Coordination: Teams must work together to barrage the central ball and force it towards an opponent's boundary line.

Station 6: Pattern Trap

Goal: A frantic Tic-Tac-Toe sprint combined with quick thinking.

Mechanics & Rules

  • Relay style: Players sprint to place 3 items in a 3x3 grid.
  • The Twist: Once 3 items are placed, runners must sprint out and move an existing item.
  • Teams constantly reposition items to block opponents and align 3-in-a-row.

Swipe/Press DOWN for Details

The Pattern Trap: Details

  • Materials: 9 Hula Hoops, 6 Colored items (3 per team).
  • Referees: 1 Ref to declare winner and reset the board.
  • Reflection Link: "Social media traps you in patterns. Sometimes we get stuck in bad habits. To win, you had to stop running blindly and break the pattern."

Reflection 1: Blind Code

Goal: Navigating a physical maze blindfolded, ignoring the "Viruses."

Mechanics & Rules

  • One player is blindfolded. Their "Programmer" stands on a chair and yells commands.
  • The Twist: Opposing team acts as "Viruses," screaming fake instructions to confuse the runner.
  • If the runner hits an obstacle, they must restart.

Swipe/Press DOWN for Details

Blind Code: Details

  • Materials: 10-15 Blindfolds, 30+ Obstacles (cones/noodles), 2 Chairs.
  • Referees: 1 Main Ref. Team Leaders act as safety spotters.
  • Reflection Link: "How hard was it to hear the right voice? The world's algorithm acts like a loud virus... What viruses do you need to mute to hear God?"

Reflection 2: The Firewall

Goal: Attack & defense game representing protecting the mind from influences.

Mechanics & Rules

  • Team A defends a middle zone (The Firewall).
  • Team B grabs "Data Packets" and sprints to cross the firewall.
  • Defense: If tagged inside the firewall, Team B must drop the packet and run back.

Swipe/Press DOWN for Details

The Firewall: Details

  • Materials: 40 "Data Packets" (tennis balls), 4 Buckets, Cones.
  • Referees: 1 Ref for time and tag calls.
  • Reflection Link: "Your mind is a firewall. The world constantly tries to push 'data packets' past your defenses. Are you an active firewall?"

Reflection 3: Viral Trend

Goal: High-energy water sponge relay to build morale and cool down.

Mechanics & Rules

  • Teams line up. Front player dunks a giant sponge in a water bucket.
  • Pass the sponge OVER the head, then next person passes UNDER the legs.
  • Last person squeezes the water into an empty bucket and runs to the front.

Swipe/Press DOWN for Details

The Viral Trend: Details

  • Materials: 2 Giant Sponges, 4 Large Buckets, Access to a hose.
  • Referees: 1 Ref to enforce over/under rule. Leaders refill water.
  • Reflection Link: "Like that sponge, we pass on what we consume. Are you passing on a viral trend of kindness, or gossip and negativity?"
